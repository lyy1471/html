应用简介 · About

宝贝积分帮家长用「积分」把日常习惯变简单：每个孩子独立一套积分名与流水，首页即可加减分；配合每日/每周任务与奖励中心，让努力看得见、兑得到。

KidPoint turns everyday habits into a clear point system. Each child has their own profile and ledger, quick logging from home, plus tasks and a reward center so progress is easy to track and celebrate.

功能说明 · Features

多孩档案与积分体系 Child profiles & point naming

添加多个孩子，随时切换；每人独立积分与记录。可自定义积分名称（例如星星、金币、积分），适配不同家庭的叫法。

Multiple child profiles with one-tap switching. Each child keeps a separate balance and history, and you can rename “points” to match your family’s style.

行为记录 Behavior logging

为好习惯与待改进项配置条目，在首页快速记录加减分；可为单项行为设置每日次数上限，避免重复刷屏。

Configure good behaviors and ones to improve, then log changes in seconds from the home screen—with optional daily caps per behavior.

任务与周期 Tasks & periods

创建每日或每周任务：设定目标次数与完成奖励；新周期开始时，可按规则处理未达标情况（例如扣分），并可配合本地提醒。

Daily or weekly tasks with targets and bonuses; when a new period starts, you can apply rules for misses (e.g. penalties), with optional local reminders.

奖励中心 Reward center

兑换型奖励：设定所需积分，孩子攒够即可兑换；里程碑：当总积分达到目标时可领取庆祝。兑换与领取记录可查。

Redeemable rewards with point costs, plus milestones when totals hit your targets—history stays visible for both.

统计与明细 Stats & history

查看积分趋势（如多日走势）并按类型浏览流水，方便家长和孩子一起复盘「这周哪里做得好」。

Trends over time and filterable ledgers so you can review progress together.

数据导出与安全 Export & your data

支持将数据导出为文件备份与再次导入；也可在支持的设备上使用系统提供的云或文件方式做备份（以应用内实际选项为准）。

Export and import backups when you want a copy off-device; cloud/file options depend on what the app offers on your device.
