关于本应用

找找看（英文应用名 ColorClimb）是一款益智类休闲游戏：在由色块组成的网格中，找出那一块在色彩上略有不同的方块。规则简单，却能持续调动你的注意力、观察力与色彩分辨能力。

在反复辨认细微色差的过程中，大脑需要快速比较、筛选与决策，有助于锻炼视觉工作记忆与反应判断，适合作为日常脑力放松与轻度认知训练——无论是学生课余、上班族休息，还是希望保持思维敏捷的长者，都可以用几分钟完成一局「轻量健脑」小练习。

游戏提供多种难度与模式，关卡推进时挑战逐步升级，鼓励你在专注与耐心之间找到节奏，享受由易到难、不断突破自我的成就感。

益智与认知方面的意义

  • 观察力：在相似刺激中锁定差异，训练细节捕捉能力。
  • 专注力：短时高度集中，有助于提升持续注意的品质。
  • 色彩与空间知觉：在二维网格中整合颜色信息，促进视觉辨别。
  • 决策与反应：在压力下快速判断正误，锻炼思维敏捷度。
English

About this app

ColorClimb is a puzzle game: among a grid of colored tiles, find the one whose color is slightly different. The rules are simple, yet the task steadily engages your attention, observation, and color discrimination.

Spotting subtle differences again and again encourages the brain to compare, filter, and decide quickly—supporting visual working memory and responsive judgment. It works well as a short daily session for mental refreshment and light cognitive exercise, whether you are a student, a busy professional, or anyone who enjoys keeping their mind sharp.

Multiple difficulties and modes let the challenge grow as you progress, helping you balance focus and patience while enjoying the satisfaction of steady improvement.

Why it’s good for your mind

  • Observation: practice noticing small differences among similar patterns.
  • Focus: short bursts of concentration can strengthen sustained attention.
  • Color perception: integrate hue and brightness cues on a 2D grid.
  • Decision-making: respond quickly under light time pressure.
